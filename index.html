Engineering Design Firm

GH BUILDING DESIGNERS

GH Building Designers is an Engineering Design Firm in Ontario focused on providing building designs and permit application services for residential and commercial buildings in Ontario.

GH Building Designers offer a one stop shop for all your engineering and architectural design needs right from concept to obtaining the final municipal/ city approvals! We engage in different types of engineering and architectural design work for Basement Unit Conversions, Interior Alterations, Load Bearing Wall Removals, Building Extensions, Garden Suites, Land Severances and new building projects.
