GH BUILDING DESIGNERS

GH Property Management Inc. is an Engineering Design Firm in Ontario focused on providing engineering design services, field investigations and supervision of construction / renovation of residential and commercial properties in Ontario.

GH Property Management Inc. offers a one stop shop for all your engineering and architectural design needs right from concept to obtaining the final municipal/ city approvals! We engage in different types of engineering and architectural design work like Tiny Homes (Garden Suites), Basement Unit Conversion, Interior Alterations, Load Bearing Wall Removal, Land Severances, Building Extensions, New Building Construction and Land Severances.
